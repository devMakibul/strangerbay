Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 17, 2026

Your privacy is our utmost priority. Because StrangerBay is designed around anonymity, our privacy policy is straightforward and simple.

Anonymity First

We do not require you to create an account, nor do we ask for your email, phone number, location, or real name. Your identity remains anonymous throughout your use of the service.

Message Privacy

Conversation content on StrangerBay is private. We do not monitor your private chats for advertising purposes, and we do not sell your conversation data to third parties.

Data Retention

To ensure a safe and high-performing environment, chat data is automatically and permanently deleted from our systems at regular intervals. Once a chat session is ended by either user, the connection is closed immediately.

No Third-Party Sharing

We do not share or sell any of your information or metadata to third-party data brokers, advertisers, or any other external entities.

Cookies

We may use essential cookies or local storage to maintain your chat session and preferences. These are not used for tracking your behavior across other websites.
