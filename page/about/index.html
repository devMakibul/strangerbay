About StrangerBay

Last Updated: March 17, 2026

StrangerBay is a platform designed for anonymous conversations. We believe that in a world where everything is tracked, having a space to talk freely without judgement or permanence is essential.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide a safe, secure, and fast anonymous chat experience where users can connect with people from all over the world without the need for accounts or identity verification.

How it Works

When you click "Start Chatting", you are paired with another live user from somewhere in the world. The process is completely random. As soon as either of you ends the chat or closes the browser, the connection is instantly severed and all messages vanish from the system.

Privacy First

Our philosophy is built on privacy. We don't build user profiles, we don't sell data, and we don't track who you are. We've created StrangerBay to be the kind of space the original internet was — a place for open, anonymous, and genuine exchange.
